Assignment 1: Explore Hillsboro

Hillsboro is a beautiful city outside of Portland. Bellow you can see notable buildings in Hillsboro marked. Further down the page their are fixed maps exploring each of the areas in detail.

Here we can see Hillsboro's main street. The blue markers mark major community buildings in the area. Green marks government buildings. This downtown is a populer spot for shoping and theater.

Here we can see the Hillsboro Airport. The markers in red are inforstructure related to the airport. The Hillsboro Airshow is hosted here every year. Learn more
